# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team.

## Enforcement Guidelines
The project team will review and investigate reports and take any action they deem appropriate.
